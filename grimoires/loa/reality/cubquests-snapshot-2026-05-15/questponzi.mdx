## Overview

50% of the $BERA we get from badge mints goes into the season treasury.

## Reward Mechanics

When season is concluded, the $BERA in the season treasury is used to purchase rewards.
Those rewards are then raffled among an X amount of owners of the season badges.

The total amount of badges the user owns works as the ticket/odds multiplier.
